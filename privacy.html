Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information — in plain language

Last updated: 13 May 2026

PageTurn ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes what we collect when you use this website (our "Site"), why we collect it, and the choices you have. If anything here isn't clear, please reach out — we're happy to talk it through.

Information we collect

When you browse or shop

We automatically receive certain technical information, such as your browser type, general device information, and pages you visit on our Site. This helps us keep the shop running smoothly and understand what readers enjoy.

When you create an account

If you register or sign in, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, email address, and any profile information you choose to share. Authentication and account storage for our Site are handled by Google Firebase on our behalf.

When you place an order

We collect what's needed to fulfil your order: delivery address, contact details, and payment information processed by our payment partners (we do not store your full card number on our own servers).

Shopping cart

Items you add to your cart may be stored locally in your browser (local storage) so your basket survives a refresh. That data stays on your device until you clear it or check out.

Newsletters & messages

If you subscribe to our newsletter or use our contact form, we collect your email address and any message content you send so we can respond.

How we use your information

  • To process and deliver orders, and to communicate about your purchases.
  • To operate and improve the Site, including security, troubleshooting, and analytics.
  • To send marketing emails when you've agreed to receive them — you can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and those of our customers.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers who help us run PageTurn (for example hosting, email delivery, payments, and Firebase authentication), and only as needed for those services. We require them to protect your data appropriately.

Cookies & similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies where necessary for the Site to function, to keep you signed in, and to remember preferences. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended if you disable them.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes above — for example, order records for accounting and legal requirements, or account data while your account remains open. When data is no longer required, we delete or anonymise it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, email us at JonDoe@gmail.com. We'll respond within a reasonable time.

Children's privacy

Our Site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe we have done so in error, please contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

International visitors

PageTurn is based in the United States. If you visit from elsewhere, your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. or other countries where our providers operate. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards apply.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to check back occasionally — your continued use of the Site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Questions about privacy? Write to JonDoe@gmail.com or use our contact form. We'd rather answer you directly than leave you guessing.
