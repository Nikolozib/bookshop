Returns & Refunds

Shopping for books should feel effortless — including when something isn't quite right

Last updated: 13 May 2026

At PageTurn, we want every parcel to arrive as carefully chosen as the books inside it. If your order doesn't meet that standard, we'll make it right — kindly, clearly, and without fuss.

Our 30-day promise

You may return most new, unread books within 30 days of delivery for a full refund of the item price. We ask only that copies remain in the condition you received them — no dog-ears, coffee rings, or surprise annotations (charming as they may be).

What we can refund

  • Books that arrive damaged, defective, or incorrect — we'll replace them or refund you, and we'll cover the return postage.
  • Change-of-heart returns on eligible titles within 30 days, provided they're unread and in resaleable condition.

What we can't refund

  • Digital products, gift cards, or items marked final sale at checkout.
  • Books that show signs of use, missing pages, or removed shrink-wrap where applicable.

How to start a return

  1. Email us at JonDoe@gmail.com with your order number and a brief description of the issue.
  2. We'll reply within one business day with a return authorisation and the next steps.
  3. Pack the book securely (original packaging helps, but a sturdy box works just as well) and send it using the instructions we provide.

Refunds & timing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund to the original payment method. Please allow 5–10 business days for the funds to appear — banks vary, and we're not always as quick as we'd like, either.

If you paid with a gift card or store credit, we'll restore the balance to your PageTurn account.

Return shipping

For damaged, faulty, or incorrect items, we cover return shipping. For change-of-heart returns, return postage is the customer's responsibility unless we specify otherwise in your return email.

Questions? We're only an email away — visit our Contact page or write to JonDoe@gmail.com and a real human on our team will help.
